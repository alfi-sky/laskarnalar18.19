DETERMINERS

1. Determiners

Determiners adalah kata yang 'mengidentifikasi sebuah kata benda'.

These .....
Those ....
This Is this your phone
That .......
Some .....

2. Possesive Determiners

Possesive Determiners adalah kata yang mengidentifikasikan kepemilikan

My This is my book
His He always need his best friends
Her Her bag was stolen by unpredicted people
Your What's your problem
Our We always need to develop our research capability
Their Singapore's Government really always develep their education
Its .........

3. Article

Beberapa contoh article antara lain, A, an, & the

The Indefinite Article

An, digunakan ketika 'noun' berawalan huruf vocal : An alien
A, digunakan ketika 'noun' berawalan huruf konsonan : I see a book on your bag

NOTE:

1. Kita tidak menggunakan Article sebelum Proper noun

I watch a spiderman 3. Itu tidak benar, tapi: I watch Spiderman 3

The Definite Article

The Definite Article hanya kata the. The digunakan sebelum kata benda spesifik.

The definite article kadang digunakan setelah 'kata benda', namun kata benda tersebut telah diperkenalkan sebelumnya.

I got a book yesterday. The book describes how science work.

2. kita bisa menggunakan the definite article (bukan indefinite article) sebelum 'adjective'.

The Minister of Education and Culture have big program for the poor

3. Abstract noun seperti 'love', 'politic' itu tidak bisa menggunakan article tapi untuk 'concrete noun' kita bisa menggunakan article (the definite article),

All you need is love . it's correct

Can you see the water ?
