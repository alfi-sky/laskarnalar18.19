PRONOUN

Sederhananya, Pronoun disebut dengan kata ganti.

Pronoun terbagi menjadi 5 tipe

1. Subject Pronoun

Subject Pronoun atau kata ganti subjek muncul sebelum kata kerja utama

They are I, You, We, They, She, He, It

I need your jacket

We are friends

You are the best student

They look on the sky last night

He plays bandinton every week

She reads book every night

It's a great motorcycle

2. Object Pronoun

Object Pronoun atau Kata ganti objek datang setelah kata kerja utama

Me The Duck attacked me
Us The Supervisor gave their knowledge for us
You Your girlfriends loves you
Them Teroris try to kill them
Him A snake bit him
Her Have you seen her?
It I have lost it

3. Reflexive Pronoun

Ketika subjek dan objek sama. maka kita menggunakan reflexive pronoun

My Self I look at My Self on the mirror
Ourselves We on going to bring ourselves to Yogyakarta cause our victory
Yourself You must keep Yourself for me
Themselves Their's bad activity to environment kill themselves
Himself
Herself
Itself

4. Possesive Pronoun

Possesive pronoun digunakan untuk menunjukkan kepemilikan dari benda di sebuah kalimat

Mine
Yours
His
Hers
Ours
Theirs

NOTICE

Kita tidak bisa membuat Possesive Noun dari It

5. Relative Pronoun

Relative Pronoun memberikan kita informasi tambahan terkait objek atau subjek dari sebuah kalimat

Who
Whom
Which
That
Where

DANGER

What is never relative pronoun.
